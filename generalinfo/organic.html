Organic teas and botanicals details

Chamomile

Known for its calming properties, chamomile tea is often used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality.

Peppermint

Peppermint tea has a refreshing and minty flavor. It is commonly consumed for its soothing effects on the digestive system and potential to relieve headaches.

Ginger

Ginger tea has a spicy and warming taste. It is popular for its potential anti-inflammatory properties and its ability to aid digestion and alleviate nausea.

Lavender

Lavender tea is known for its floral aroma and relaxing effects. It is often used to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve sleep.

Lemon Balm

Lemon balm tea has a citrusy flavor and is believed to have calming and stress-relieving properties. It is also used to support digestive health.

Echinacea

Echinacea tea is made from the flowers and leaves of the Echinacea plant. It is commonly consumed to support the immune system and reduce the severity and duration of cold symptoms.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus tea has a tart and fruity taste. It is rich in antioxidants and is often consumed for its potential to support heart health and lower blood pressure.

Rosehip

Rosehip tea is made from the fruit of the rose plant. It is known for its high vitamin C content and is often consumed for its potential immune-boosting properties.

Lemongrass

Lemongrass tea has a citrusy and herbal flavor. It is refreshing and is commonly consumed for its potential digestive benefits and calming effects.
