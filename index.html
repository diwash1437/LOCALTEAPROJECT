About Us

The art of local tea is a refined practice that involves cultivating tea in specific regions, each with its unique environmental conditions and climate. Tea cultivation is passed down through generations, with tea masters sharing their knowledge and skills. Meticulous attention is given to every step of the process, from plucking the young, tender leaves to carefully processing them through various methods like withering, rolling, oxidation, and drying.
